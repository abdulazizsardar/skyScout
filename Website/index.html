Retningslinjer for informasjonskapsler

Like most websites on the Internet, StaffCloud.io uses cookies to make everything you do and we do in our browsers work together seamlessly.

A cookie is a small text file which our server will place onto your computer's hard drive in the section your Web browser uses for exactly this purpose. The file is added and the cookie helps StaffCloud’s servers know who you are, among other similar functions. 

Cookies allow web applications, like the one StaffCloud uses, to respond to each person who uses the site and its services individually. This allows the StaffCloud servers to give you only the information you need and not anything that isn’t relevant to you and the work you do.

In a nutshell, the use of cookies allows StaffCloud to do what it does, which allows you to do what it is you do. They don’t give us access to any part of your computer except as noted above, and even then it serves almost exclusively as an identifier.

StaffCloud will not use cookies for advertising or marketing analytics. We may use them for internal performance analytics, such as counting the number of visitors a particular page gets in a given time frame. In such a case the cookies will not disclose any personally identifying information about you.
